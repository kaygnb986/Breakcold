## Breakcold Introduction

Breakcold is an AI-Native CRM designed for small and medium businesses, particularly agencies, startups, and consultants who don't want to waste their days on data entry. The platform works autonomously to handle the tedious parts of sales while you focus on what actually matters: building relationships and closing deals.

Here's what makes it different. Instead of forcing you to manually update pipelines or set reminders, Breakcold's AI watches your interactions and does it for you. Email a lead? The CRM moves them forward. Have a LinkedIn conversation? It logs that automatically. Miss a follow-up? The system creates a task without you lifting a finger.

The platform combines traditional CRM features with social selling capabilities. You get contact management, sales pipelines, and email tracking—but also a unified feed of your prospects' LinkedIn and Twitter activity. So when someone in your pipeline posts about a challenge your product solves, you'll see it immediately. That's the kind of timing that turns cold leads into warm conversations.

Breakcold monitors your interactions with leads and automatically creates follow-up tasks so you never lose momentum. During onboarding, they help you build sales pipelines tailored to your specific business situation. The AI detects duplicate contacts and can merge them while keeping the best information from each record. Based on your email and social interactions, the system automatically assigns scores to your leads.

Over 1,000 agencies, startups, and consultants are already using Breakcold to streamline their sales processes. The platform integrates with LinkedIn, Twitter, email, WhatsApp, and Telegram—giving you one place to manage all your outreach. Whether you're a solo consultant or a growing sales team, Breakcold scales with your needs.

**Understanding AI Leads**

AI Leads are leads where the AI is actively running. You can reallocate your AI leads as needed—you don't need AI running on every contact all the time. The key advantage is that you get unlimited AI actions on your AI Leads, which is significantly cheaper than the credit-based systems other CRMs use.

**What's Included Across All Plans**

Every Breakcold subscription includes unlimited contacts with custom fields, unlimited sales pipelines and deals, activity history tracking, collaborative notes, reminders and tasks, email inbox integration, and access to the API, Zapier, and webhooks. The CRM automatically moves leads based on your interactions, creates tasks for follow-ups, handles pipeline creation during onboarding, detects and merges duplicate contacts, and assigns lead scores based on engagement.

**Choosing the Right Plan**

If you're a solo consultant or freelancer just starting with social CRM, the Essentials plan gives you everything you need to manage up to 25 active AI-powered leads. The Pro plan is ideal for growing teams that need more integration options, want to sync one social platform automatically, and require unlimited analytics to track performance. The Max plan is built for agencies or sales teams that live on multiple social platforms and need the highest level of automation and priority support.

What are real users saying about Breakcold? The feedback paints an interesting picture.

The standout feature according to reviews is the lead management capability. Reviewers appreciate the ability to connect and nurture leads across LinkedIn, Twitter, and email. The social media integration is particularly valuable, allowing seamless connection with LinkedIn and Twitter. Many reviewers note this integration sets Breakcold apart from traditional CRM platforms.

One AppSumo user captured the sentiment well: "I've been using BreakCold CRM for just a few days, and it's quickly becoming a favorite! What I absolutely love is the pipeline view, where I can see the status of each lead and what actions to take—all at a glance."

**Areas for Improvement**

Not everything is perfect. Some users highlight missing features, including limitations on platform integrations and usage restrictions. A few reviewers report occasional connectivity issues, particularly with LinkedIn, which can complicate workflows.

There have been some concerning reports about customer service and billing practices. A small number of users mentioned issues with subscription renewals and difficulties resolving license activation problems. While these appear to be isolated cases, they're worth noting.

Some users mentioned they'd like to see more automation features and the ability to reply to LinkedIn messages directly within the same screen. Support during the trial period has also been called out as an area that could improve.

**The Verdict from Users**

Overall, Breakcold is praised for its effectiveness in managing CRM and social media outreach, with users highlighting its ability to replace multiple tools like Sales Navigator, lemlist, and HubSpot at a lower cost. Users appreciate the seamless integration of LinkedIn and email campaigns, noting high open rates and the ability to track social media activity.

The platform has received recognition from major review platforms including G2 and Capterra, where it earned "Best Value SMBs CRM" and was included in the "CRM Shortlist 2024."

For agencies, consultants, and startups focused on LinkedIn-based selling, Breakcold seems to deliver on its promise of simplifying social CRM. The automation saves time, the interface is clean, and the social selling features genuinely help with prospecting. Just be aware that, like any software, it's not perfect for everyone—especially if you need deep customization or are heavily invested in platforms beyond LinkedIn and Twitter.
